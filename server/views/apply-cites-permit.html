Apply for a CITES permit

Use this service to apply for a permit to import, export or re-export under the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES).

You can use this service to apply for a permit to:

  • import, export or re-export a specimen
  • use a specimen for commercial purposes (also called an Article 10)
